Romantic Rose

I created a monogram collage centered around a botanical illustration of a red rose.

The color palette of the collage creates a direct complementary harmony.

The illustration has an asymmetrical balance, where the leaves and the flower are positioned on the right side of the illustration. The layout of the illustration is dynamic since the stem of the rose creates diagonal leading lines.
