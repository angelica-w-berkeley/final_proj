UMBS Banners

I worked conceptualizing a set of posters for various classes offered by the University of Michigan Biological Station (UMBS).

This involved creating unity throughout all the posters by making sure the images and color palette were in harmonious agreement and form a conceptual color theme, as well as maintaining a balanced composition for each poster.

For the color palette, I decided to use various hues of blue and green to create an analogous harmony. For images, I used Unsplash to find pictures that fit the theme of each course, and then I edited the colors for all of them so that they would look more cohesive.
